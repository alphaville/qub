Certainty-Equivalent Control

Worst-case Control

Worst-case MPC:
$$ \operatorname*{Minimise}_{u_0,\ldots,u_{N-1}} \operatorname*{max}_{w_0,\ldots, w_{N-1}} \left[ \ell_N(x_N) + \sum_{t=0}^{N-1} \ell_t(x_t, u_t, w_t)\right] $$
subject to the system dynamics and constraints.

Stochastic Control

Stochastic MPC:
$$ \operatorname*{Minimise}_{u_0,\ldots,u_{N-1}} \mathbb{E}_{w_0,\ldots, w_{N-1}} \left[ \ell_N(x_N) + \sum_{t=0}^{N-1} \ell_t(x_t, u_t, w_t)\right] $$
subject to the system dynamics and constraints.
